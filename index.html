Ingredients

  • Chicken - 1 whole cooked chicken
  • Yellow Onions - 3 medium
  • Carrots - 4 large
  • Celery - 2 bundles
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Delicious Chicken Soup

Description

This is a simple yet delicious recipe for making chicken soup. Get ready to enjoy!

Directions

  1. Debone the chicken: Cut off the breats, thighs, legs and wings. Keep these aside for later. Place the remainder of the chichen in a large pot and add water. Bring this to a boil and let it boil for 2 hours.
  2. Take your carrots, onions and celery. Peel the carrots and cut the into small circles. Wash and cut the celery into small 1/2 inch pieces. Cut the onions into your preferred length.
  3. Use a strainer to remove the chicken bones from the broth. Add the celery and carrots to the broth and bring to a simmer. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Use a separate pan to cook the onions until they are translucent. Then add them to the broth.
  5. Cut the chicken into your desired piece sizes, and add to the broth as well. Let the broth simmer for 2-3 hours.
